**Basile Fraboni** About ==============================================================

PhD student at LIRIS and INSA Lyon in the [Origami](https://projet.liris.cnrs.fr/origami/index.html) and Imagine groups. My research area focuses on computer graphics and realistic rendering. I also play along with random stuff like discrete optimal transport, Voronoi diagrams, optimization and algorithmic art. **Contact** [basile.fraboni@insa-lyon.fr](basile.fraboni@insa-lyon.fr) [basile.fraboni@univ-lyon1.fr](basile.fraboni@univ-lyon1.fr) [Github](https://github.com/bfraboni) [Twitter](https://twitter.com/BasileFraboni)
